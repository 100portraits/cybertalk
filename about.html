Our Project

We are a group of Computational Social Science students at University of Amsterdam, who have worked together for a span of four months on a research project, which is:

How can primary school teachers be encouraged to implement innovative teaching methods like gamification in the classroom?

The research was formed in collaboration with our project partner - HackShield. They have developed an educational game for children to learn about cybersecurity and introduced us to the topic of gamification, which led us to studying the use of innovative digital teaching techniques. By conducting a literature review, a focus group with teachers and educational experts, and A/B testing, we have come up with this intervention.

Through this website, we are hoping to not only help teachers around the world to make their classes more engaging, but also initiate the needed change in the educational system to be more on pace with the on-going technological development.
