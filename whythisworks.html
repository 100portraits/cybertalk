Why This Works

Since the start of digitalisation, many aspects of daily life has been replaced or supported by digital tools, such as communication, financial transactions, and researching. It has become a natural part of our lives, and we believe that children should learn how to use it.

Here are the facts:

Teacher engagement

is positively related to

student engagement[1]

Students' attention spans can get as low as

30 seconds

close to the end of a traditional lecture[2]

Gamification creates up to a

40%

increase in students' ability to learn new skills[3]
